Author and Source Completion

An Author and Source Completion button now appears while typing an author or source. Tapping this + button will allow you to search for pre-existing authors and sources.

1.2 (Ostia)


New

Import/Export

Quotebook now imports and exports text files and CSV files. It even supports using custom CSV files! To make a CSV file from your computer, name the columns: Quote, Author, Source, Date, Rating, and Tags. Then save as UTF-8 CSV and transfer through iTunes file sharing.

New

URL and Twitter Detection

When an Author or Source field contains a URL, the blue arrow will open the web page instead of Wikipedia. If it contains a Twitter username like @username, it will open that user's Twitter profile.

New

Copy from Share Button

Tapping the share button while viewing a quote now provides the option to Copy to clipboard.

Fixed

Quote area now has a shadow to differentiate it from other fields.


Fixed

Issue with Auto-Suggest button improperly resizing.


Fixed

Alerts now include images.


Fixed

Shared quotes are now in the format "Quote" – Author in Source.


Fixed

Improved performance.


Fixed

Updated Legal text.


Fixed

Rare bug with Auto-Detection

An Auto-Detected quote author typed on the device followed by an even number of spaces would not properly detect a source. Due to iOS inserting an invisible character with any even amount of spaces typed on the device.

1.1 (Skynet)


New

Auto-Suggest


  • Try adding a famous quote to Quotebook. Can't remember the whole thing? Just use Auto-Suggest. Quotebook can now figure out the author, source, and even tags of famous quotations.
  • Auto-Suggest isn't designed to search for new quotes. It uses the internet to complete quotes that you've come across or can't remember, saving you time and effort. Think of it as a tiny literary butler inside your iPhone.
  • Try a few to get started: “Be the change…” “One small step…” “It's kind of fun…”

New

Enhanced Auto-Detection

  • Auto-Detection alerts you to new quotes on your clipboard and lets you add them instantly.
  • It now detects the author and source of a copied quote in the form: "Quote" – Author in Source and can detect quotes from within large blocks of text.
  • Auto-Detection can now be used from right within Quotebook's Wikipedia and Wikiquote screens and now allows quotes of longer length.

Fixed

Intermittent Facebook posting problems.


Fixed

Improved error detection and crash reporting.


Fixed

Notifications upon logout of services in settings.


Fixed

Improved support for offline posting to Twitter, Tumblr, and Facebook.


Fixed

An iOS 4.3 display bug affecting the deletion of quotes.


Fixed

Issue where Auto-Detection would prompt for the same quote multiple times.


Fixed

Made Tumblr posting options consistent with other services.


1.0.1 (Engineer)


New

Quick Copy: Copy a quote to the clipboard by tapping and holding on a quote in a list.



New

The quote viewing area has increased from a maximum of 4 lines to 10.



Fixed

"Incorrect Signature" error when posting to Twitter.


Fixed

Bug when clicking segmented control with no quotes on 4.3.


Fixed

Bug where the date would not immediately appear upon creation of a new quote.


Fixed

Very rare bug where information fields would appear in the wrong area.

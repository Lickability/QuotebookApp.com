Quotebook

a notebook for your quotes

 

Quotebook is an iOS app for collecting and sharing the quotes that matter to you.

 

Super Fast

Capture a new quote within seconds, including the author and source.

One Tap Sharing

Send a quote via Email, SMS, Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr with one tap.

Auto-Detection

Alerts you to new quotes on your clipboard and lets you add them instantly.

Quote Discovery

Discover more quotes from your favorite authors and sources on the web.

Tags and Ratings

Organize your quotes by categories and star ratings.

Full Text Search

Search all of your quotes, authors, sources, and tags from anywhere.

Wikimedia-Powered

Instantly find out more about any author or source from Wikipedia and Wikiquote.

Export

Backup, export, and email your quotes from within Quotebook.

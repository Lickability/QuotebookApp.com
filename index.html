Who uses Quotebook?

Writers use Quotebook to record and organize quotes to use in their work. Random quotes provide daily inspiration.

Students use Quotebook to organize quotes for papers. Author bios are automatically downloaded from Wikipedia.

Everyone can use Quotebook to keep track of quotes from friends and family and lines from their favorite films.
